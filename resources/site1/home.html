Making entrepreneurial dreams come true

For over a decade our team has helped entrepreneurs launch and grow successful businesses in a variety of industries. Along the way we’ve learned what aspiring entrepreneurs need to succeed with their new ventures.

Our latest innovation in this successful line of business systems is TapSnap – a unique and fun photo and social media platform for events of all sizes.

We have an invested interest in helping our franchisees grow and bring TapSnap’s distinctive brand of event entertainment to everyone, one smile at a time.

 
Love what you do

How would you like to make complementary income by bringing joy into people’s lives? So much more than a simple photo booth, TapSnap is always the life of any party. Guests naturally gravitate towards its interactive touch screen and fun features.

Imagine owning a business that puts smiles on people’s faces, that’s backed by one of the world’s most recognized and loved brands, and that values the importance of having fun, giving back, and getting involved in the community.

Don’t believe us? Check out this video of “Happy” Andi Grupa, a Missouri franchisee, on how fun and rewarding a TapSnap event can be.

 
We’re looking for the right fit

We think big and we hope you do too. We want franchisee partners with the drive and passion to make TapSnap the number one name in event entertainment. In return, we’ll be there for you every step of the way.

We think we’ll be a good match if you…

  • Like people and enjoy the excitement of social events and parties.
  • Are a disciplined self-starter with a high level of personal integrity who prides themselves on delivering the absolute best in customer service.
  • Possess strong interpersonal and communication skills backed by the desire to build and maintain long-term client relationships.
  • Demonstrate a strong circle of influence in the franchise territory.
  • Have proven organizational and scheduling abilities, as well as the ability to adhere to the guidelines and standards of the TapSnap franchise system.
  • Can get started within 60 days.
  • Have the capital to invest in the business, and the financial support to see the business through the start-up phase.
 
You’ll have a team behind you

Franchising is the perfect route if you have the entrepreneurial spirit but would like to benefit from someone else’s proven experience.

TapSnap has over 140 franchisees across North America, so we’ve seen what works and what doesn’t. We’ll give you the tools you’ll need to successfully build your business: procedures, best practices, sales and product training, marketing support, and software – all designed to make your business efficient and profitable.
